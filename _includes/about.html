About Margaret

Growing up in the former Soviet Union, Margaret was inspired by the hard work of her parents and surrounding family to produce the best quality work. Academically inclined from a young age, Margaret subsequently fell in love with logic games and puzzles and decidedly pursued a PhD in mathematics. Upon arriving to the United States, she saw the opportunity to apply her love of mathematics by helping people find their right home.

Licensed for 26 years, Margaret has sold hundreds of properties in Virginia and DC. Primarily active in Northern Virginia, where she resides with her loving husband Lev, she has experienced the market in its highs and lows. For inquiries on endorsements and recommendations, please do not hesitate to contact.
